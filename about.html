About Us

E-LOOD CONSULTING ENGINEERS

E-Lood Consulting Engineers Company has been established at 2006 in Tehran.
The company is certificated for designing residential, industrial, sport complex and health care and hospital projects in IRAN.
E-Lood is consisted of 6 departments of engineering as follows:
1- Architectural
2- Structural and Civil
3- Mechanical
4- Electerical
5- IT
6- Management on top supervision and project
E- Lood Consulting Engineers has a grade 1 engineering degree (the highest grade for consulting engineers in IRAN) from the Iranian management and planning organization and has designed and managed many projects, and the most important international project of this company is the Chaka Residential Commercial Complex in Iddis Ababa, the capital of Ethiopia.
